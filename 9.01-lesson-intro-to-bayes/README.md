## Learning Objectives

*After this lesson, students will be able to:*

1. Understand Bayes Theorem and how it's derived.
2. Know how joint probability plays a role in Bayes Theorem
3. Understand the Lay of Total Probability
4. Use Bayes Theorem to solve the Monty Hall problem

## Lesson Outline

> **Total Time: 90 mins**

I. **Intro to Bayes** (90 minutes total)
- Review joint probability
- Derive Bayes Theorem
- Law of Total Probability
- The cookie jar problem
- The Monty Hall problem

II. Monte Carlo Simulation
